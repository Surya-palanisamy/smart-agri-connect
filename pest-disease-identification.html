Pest and Disease Identification

Common Pests

Pest Description Symptoms
Green Leafhopper Small, green insect affecting leaves. Yellowing leaves, stunted growth.
Caterpillars Larval stage of butterflies, damaging leaves. Chewed leaves, visible larvae.
Spider Mites Microscopic pests causing damage to plant surfaces. Speckled leaves, webbing.

Common Diseases

Disease Description Symptoms
Powdery Mildew Fungal disease forming white powdery spots. White powdery coating on leaves, reduced growth.
Leaf Blight Bacterial or fungal infection causing leaf spots. Dark spots on leaves, leaf drop.
Rust Fungal disease causing reddish-brown pustules. Reddish-brown pustules on leaves, premature leaf drop.

Identification and Treatment

Issue Identification Method Treatment
Green Leafhopper Inspect plants for small, green insects and leaf damage. Use insecticidal soap or neem oil. Implement crop rotation.
Caterpillars Look for chewed leaves and visible larvae. Apply Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt) or manually remove larvae.
Spider Mites Check for fine webbing and speckled leaves. Use miticides or spray with water to dislodge mites.
Powdery Mildew Look for white powdery spots on leaves and stems. Apply fungicides and improve air circulation around plants.
Leaf Blight Check for dark spots and lesions on leaves. Use appropriate fungicides and remove infected plant parts.
Rust Inspect for reddish-brown pustules on the underside of leaves. Apply rust-resistant varieties and fungicides. Remove affected plants.
