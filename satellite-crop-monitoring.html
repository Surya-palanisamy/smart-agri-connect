Crop Health Indicators

Indicator Description Data Source Measurement
Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) Measures vegetation health and density. Satellite imagery 0.2 - 0.9 (healthy to stressed)
Leaf Area Index (LAI) Indicates the amount of leaf coverage. Satellite-based sensors 0 - 6 (minimal to full coverage)
Canopy Cover Percentage of ground covered by plant canopy. High-resolution satellite images 0% - 100%

Soil Moisture Measures the amount of water in the soil. Satellite radar 0 - 100% volumetric water content

Crop Stress Detection

Type of Stress Description Detection Method Indicators
Water Stress Caused by insufficient or excessive water. NDVI, Soil Moisture Decreased NDVI values, dry soil
Nutrient Stress Due to nutrient deficiencies in soil. Chlorophyll Content, NDVI Reduced chlorophyll content, lower NDVI
Pest Infestation Damage caused by pests. Canopy Cover, NDVI Patchy canopy cover, decreased NDVI
Disease Fungal or bacterial infections. NDVI, Canopy Cover Discolored patches, reduced canopy cover
Temperature Stress Effects of high or low temperatures. NDVI, Soil Moisture Decreased NDVI, altered soil moisture
